Visit Washington Heights!

Celebrate life in this vibrant neighborhood in Northern Manhattan

Top three activities to do in Washington Heights

See a free movie at the United Palace. The theater was built in the 1930s and recently hosted the Tony Awards!

Explore The Met's medieval art and architecture museum, The Cloisters, located in beautiful Fort Tryon Park.

Taste the world famous cuisine of Washington Heights. To eat like a local, try Mambi's cubano sandwich!
